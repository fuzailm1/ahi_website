Shaykh Okasha Kameny

Our beloved Imam Okasha Kameny, may Allah preserve and increase him in goodness, has sought to establish a platform for spreading the ‘ilm Allah has granted him. Okasha Kameny has been blessed by Allah to formally study Islam with several reputable contemporary scholars in the sciences of Arabic, Quranic Sciences (such as Al-Qira’at [the recitation forms of Qur’an] and Tafsir), Ahadith, Fiqh, Islamic History, ‘Aqeedah, Poetry, and more. He has been blessed by Allah to memorize and have ijazaat (certifications from Islamic Scholars) in the 10 Qira’at of Al-Qur’an as well as over a dozen different books ranging from Saheeh Bukhari and Muslim, Alfiyyah ibn Malik, Ash-Shatibiyyah and much more. Allah has blessed him with remarkable precision in his memory and the understanding to explain Islamic Concepts to Muslims at all levels of knowledge. With the gifts, knowledge, and experience that Allah has granted him, it is his utmost desire to exert himself in sharing these with your community with the hope that Allah makes these programs a means of securing knowledge and unity in your community for the near and distant future.

The Shaykh studied Qur’ān, Qira’āt, Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, Arabic Linguistics, Rhetoric and Poetry at the feet of prominent scholars either residing in Ghana or visiting from other Islamic centers of learning, such as Al Azhar and the Islamic University of Madina. The Shaykh was not only a student to his teachers but just as the students of the Salaf were, he was their aid and a helper. Many of his teachers recognized the Shaykh’s humility and kindness, and through their love for him they imparted knowledge to him that is beyond his age and time.
